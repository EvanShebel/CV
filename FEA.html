Pressure loads for UAV

Displacement plot
Displacement plot with a visible fine mesh.

  • Model is a simplified Underwater Autonomous Vehicle.

  • Used brick elements for solid geometry.
  • Design scenarios were used for different depths.

Rigid Boundary Elements

RBE spiders to simulate bolts. Top and bottom spiders are rigid elements with "no" mass. Interconnnecting element between the spiders is a beam element to collect reaction forces in 6 DOF
Axial stress results
Bending stress results in 3rd axis. Also showing superimposed bending moment diagram.
Von Mises results and displacement shown.

  • For beam elements there is an associated geometry to select. In this example a round profile was chosen with a .125 in diameter.
  • Shown below are the resultant forces on the beam elements representing the bolts. The x, y, z reaction forces sum to applied loads.
